Intro

I started working as a web developer in 2006 doing mainly frontend development and working with Rails (it was not that big back then) in my sparetime. In 2008 I went on working with Rails as a dayjob, improving and mainting Fluxiom. After more than 2 years I took a little detour and learned ColdFusion for work. Half a year was enough to discover that I'd rather like to work with Ruby again and started working for Tupalo.com. For the last six months I've been working for a start-up using PHP. I've always been bouncing between frontend- and backend-development and doing OPs on the side as well.
